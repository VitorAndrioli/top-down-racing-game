Detailed Description

Controllable car to be used by the player.

Implements mechanics for drivable objects, including acceleration, reverse gear, steering and braking, based on user inputs. Also implements texture allocation for cars.

Author
Vitor Augusto Andrioli
Version
1.0
Date
25/02/2016
Todo:
Add drift effect to car.
Bug:

Turning while not accelerating (forwards or backwards) gets the car stuck. Method to identify whether the car is moving forward, to adjust for bicycle steering, is defective.

The car has no sideways displacement.
