P - pause / resume

W - player 1 accelerate
S - player 1 reverse gear
F - player 1 turn right
A - player 1 turn left
Left Ctrl - player 1 brake

Arrow up - player 2 accelerate
Arrow down - player 2 reverse gear
Arrow right - player 2 turn right
Arrow left - player 2 turn left
Right Ctrl - player 2 brake
